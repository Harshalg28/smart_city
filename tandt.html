Travel Options in Solapur

  • Railway: Solapur is a major railway junction on the Central Railway.
  • Road: Connected to major cities via NH-65 and NH-52.
  • Airport: Solapur Airport (limited services); nearest major airport is Pune Airport (250 km).
  • Local Transport: Buses, auto-rickshaws, and smart city electric rickshaws.
